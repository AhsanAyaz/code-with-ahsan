Magenta Security grew from a local Hounslow company to a nationwide industry leader thanks to our close relationship with our clients. Many of our now standard services were born out of being asked for a service that we didn’t provide yet, and we continue to adapt our business based on your needs to this day.

“Never Say No” is Magenta Security’s company-wide ethos. It pushes us to go outside of our comfort zone and tear up the rule book when our clients need us to, resulting in us tackling everything from top-to-bottom event management and service delivery to international private security escorts. If you can’t find what you need on our robust list of services, just give us a call and we will make your safety our business.

This pursuit of quality has not gone unnoticed. Magenta Security are in the top 5% of SIA’s Approved Contractor Scheme, a National Security Inspectorate gold member, an Alcumus SafeContractor and was the first security company in Europe to achieve the ISO 14001 environmental standard. Managing Director Abbey Petkar is a respected figure and fierce advocate for positive change in the industry and improved conditions for the people who work in it.

A Message from Abbey Petkar, MD of Magenta Security

People have been my priority from day one. Though we’ve expanded to provide an ever-growing range of services, the core of our business has always been our security guards.

I believe a happy guard is a more dedicated guard who’s willing to go above and beyond to protect our clients, a belief that’s proven to be true time and time again. That’s why all our security guards are full-time employees who enjoy a range of benefits and a healthy work-life balance.

My people priority extends outside the company as well. Our charitable initiatives have helped vulnerable children in the UK and beyond find health and happiness, and we do our part in leaving a better world for them through our commitment to the environment and carbon neutrality.
